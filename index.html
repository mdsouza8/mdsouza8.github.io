What I'm About.

Hi! I'm Marissa. Since I was 10 years old, I have wanted to be a doctor. As I've grown up, however, I've learned the many discrepancies between the interfaces physicans and patients desire and the interfaces developers provide.

And that's where I come in.
 Combining my background in computer science, data analytics, and UI design with my increasing knowledge and behind-the-scenes perspective of the medical field, I hope to come up with user-friendly and aesthetically-pleasing solutions to help make the lives of physicians and patients everywhere just a little bit easier.

What I Know.

At Georgia Tech, I was a Computer Science major with concentrations in the People and Media threads. During my time there, I designed and built human-centered systems that utilized computing's ability to provide creative outlets. Now, at Mercer University's School of Medicine, I am expanding my knowledge to learn the intricacies of how the human body functions normally along with the pathophysiology and treatments for when it doesn't.
