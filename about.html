Hi! I'm Marissa.

I'm a third year medical student with passions for science, technology, design, and a lot of other seemingly unrelated things.

While I used to think that my interests were random and unrelated, I've grown to realize that there lies a common theme: I like to use my mind, both creatively and scientifically, to create things that make life easier, better, or happier.

Early on, I took a liking to design & aesthetics, and have grown to be particularly interested in how and why we find things beautiful. I learned to code, and became interested in how I could use technology to produce beautiful things to both please and help people. I have traveled to many countries, taking note of how both location and culture affect people and their interactions with others and the environment.

In medical school, I've taken interests in pathways and processes, whether that is quality improvement in healthcare systems or the hypothalamic-pituitary axis. That being said...


I am continuing to learn about myself and the world, and have put together this website as a means to both express myself and organize my thoughts & creations.

Hope you like it!
