Hi! I'm Marissa.

I'm a third year medical student with passions for the brain, technology, and design.

Ever since I attended a "Brain Camp" when I was 10 years old, neuroscience, technology, and the intersection of the two fields have continued to intrigue me. I obtained my undergraduate degree in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, where I learned to design, build, and evaluate human-centered systems. I am now in medical school, learning how the human brain works as well as what to do when it doesn't. With so much left unknown about this tiny organ, I hope to one day combine my medical and technological knowledge to create diagnostic and therapeutic solutions for those with neuropsychiatric conditions. I have put together this website as a means to both express myself and organize my thoughts & creations. Hope you like it!
